Seekho IPC - XV

Chapter XXA: Cruelty by Husband or Relatives of Husband (Section 498A)

Section 498A: Punishment for Any Cruelty Inflicted on a Married Woman by the Husband and His Relatives

Description

This section addresses the offence of cruelty inflicted on a married woman by her husband or his relatives. Cruelty may include physical or mental abuse or harassment.

Example 1

Legal consequences for a husband and his relatives if they are found guilty of subjecting a married woman to cruelty.

Example 2

Penalties and legal measures for cases of mental or physical abuse against a married woman by her husband and his family members.
