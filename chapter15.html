Seekho IPC - XV

Chapter XV: Offences relating to Religion (Sections 295 to 298)

Section 295: Injuring or Defiling Place of Worship with Intent to Insult the Religion of Any Class

Description

This section addresses the act of injuring or defiling a place of worship with the intention of insulting the religion of any class.

Example 1

Defacing a temple with graffiti that mocks the religious symbols and practices of that place.

Example 2

Destroying religious idols or artifacts in a place of worship to express disdain for the religion.

Section 295A: Deliberate and Malicious Acts, Intended to Outrage Religious Feelings or Any Class by Insulting Its Religion or Religious Beliefs

Description

This section covers deliberate and malicious acts intended to outrage the religious feelings of any class by insulting its religion or religious beliefs.

Example 1

Publishing derogatory remarks about a specific religion in a manner meant to provoke anger and outrage.

Example 2

Creating and distributing offensive material that targets and ridicules the religious beliefs of a community.

Section 296: Disturbing Religious Assembly

Description

This section deals with actions that disturb a religious assembly or gathering, disrupting its peaceful conduct.

Example 1

Interrupting a religious ceremony with disruptive behavior or loud noises that affect the participants.

Example 2

Engaging in acts that cause disorder during a religious procession or event, thereby disturbing the attendees.

Section 297: Trespassing on Burial Places, etc.

Description

This section concerns trespassing on burial places, cemeteries, or any other sacred or restricted areas related to the deceased.

Example 1

Unauthorized entry into a cemetery to commit vandalism or theft of items from graves.

Example 2

Disturbing or desecrating burial sites or tombs without permission, causing offense to the families of the deceased.

Section 298: Uttering Words, etc., with Deliberate Intent to Wound the Religious Feelings of Any Person

Description

This section addresses the act of uttering words or making statements with the deliberate intent to wound the religious feelings of any individual.

Example 1

Making disparaging remarks about an individual's religion in a manner intended to offend and hurt.

Example 2

Publicly using derogatory language or statements aimed at insulting someone's religious beliefs.
